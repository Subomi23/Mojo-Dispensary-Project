    Our Philosophy
    Cannabis Products
    Made in Missouri
    All of our products come from the finest marijuana
    growers and manufactures within the state.
    Kept fresh
    We work every day to ensure all of our flower is
    kept at the right relative humidity before leaving
    our store.
    Onsite
    Pharmacist
    To better the patient experience we offer an in-house
    Pharmacist for private consultations. Best used for
    drug on drug interactions, and to find the right product
    to cater to your medical needs.
    Locally Lab Tested
    Each individual product that comes from the
    manufacturers or cultivation goes though local
    vigorous lab testing before reaching our shelves.
    Drive-through
    Our drive-up window allows patients with mobile
    impairments to have access without leaving the seat of
    their car. It's also great for any patient that is running
    short on time.
