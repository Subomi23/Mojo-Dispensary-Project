Who We Are
We are a small, locally owned dispensary here to serve the medical needs of Nixa and our surrounding area. We have on staff an in-house pharmacist who will be available for face to face consultations.

Our Philosophy

Cannabis Products Made in Missouri

All of our products come from the finest marijuana growers and manufactures within the state.

Kept Fresh

We work every day to ensure all of our flower is kept at the right relative humidity before leaving our store.

Onsite Pharmacist

To better the patient experience we offer an in-house Pharmacist for private consultations. Best used for drug on drug interactions, and to find the right product to cater to your medical needs.

Locally Lab Tested

Each individual product that comes from the manufacturers or cultivation goes though local vigorous lab testing before reaching our shelves.

Drive-Through

Our drive-up window allows patients with mobile impairments to have access without leaving the seat of their car. It's also great for any patient that is running short on time.
